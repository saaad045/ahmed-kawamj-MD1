About Dr. Ahmed Kawamj, MD

Expert Care You Can Trust

Dr. Ahmed Kawamj is a board-certified Internal Medicine physician with advanced fellowship training in Addiction Medicine. He completed his residency in New Jersey and pursued specialized fellowship training at Wellstar MCG in Georgia, equipping him to address the growing challenges of substance use disorders.

Passionate about bridging the gap between primary care and addiction treatment, Dr. Kawamj provides comprehensive, compassionate, and patient-centered care. His experience spans both affluent and underserved communities, giving him a unique perspective on health disparities and their impact on patient outcomes.
