Why Preventive Care Matters

Taking proactive steps today can significantly impact your health tomorrow.

At Advanced Internal Medicine, we believe that prevention is the foundation of long-term health. Our comprehensive preventive care program is designed to identify potential health issues before they become serious, giving you the best chance for effective treatment and maintaining your quality of life.

Through regular check-ups, advanced screenings, and personalized health assessments, we create a proactive healthcare plan tailored to your unique needs, age, family history, and lifestyle factors. Our approach focuses on early detection, risk reduction, and health optimization.

Our Preventive Services

Comprehensive screenings and assessments for optimal health maintenance.

Comprehensive Physical Exams

Thorough annual evaluations to assess your overall health status and identify potential concerns.

  • Complete health history review
  • Vital signs assessment
  • Body systems examination
  • Personalized health recommendations

Cancer Screenings

Early detection protocols for various types of cancer based on your risk factors.

  • Mammography for breast cancer
  • Colonoscopy for colorectal cancer
  • PSA testing for prostate cancer
  • Skin cancer evaluations
  • Lung cancer screening for high-risk patients

Cardiovascular Assessments

Comprehensive heart health evaluations to prevent cardiovascular diseases.

  • Cholesterol and lipid profiling
  • Blood pressure monitoring
  • Electrocardiogram (EKG)
  • Cardiac risk assessment
  • Peripheral artery disease screening

Metabolic Health Screening

Assessment of metabolic function to prevent diabetes and related conditions.

  • Blood glucose testing
  • HbA1c for diabetes screening
  • Thyroid function tests
  • Kidney and liver function assessments
  • Nutritional status evaluation

Bone Density & Joint Health

Evaluations to maintain skeletal health and prevent osteoporosis.

  • DEXA bone density scans
  • Vitamin D level assessment
  • Calcium metabolism evaluation
  • Arthritis risk assessment
  • Fall prevention strategies

Vaccinations & Immunizations

Age-appropriate vaccines to protect against preventable diseases.

  • Influenza vaccines
  • Pneumonia vaccines
  • Shingles vaccination
  • Tetanus boosters
  • Travel vaccinations

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Explore our most frequently asked questions about preventive care — clear, simple, and designed to guide your health journey.

Preventive care includes regular check-ups, screenings, and counseling designed to help you stay healthy and detect potential health problems early, when they are most treatable.

Most adults should schedule a preventive visit once a year. However, frequency may vary depending on your age, family history, and medical conditions.

Many insurance plans cover preventive care services at no cost to you. This typically includes physical exams, screenings, and vaccinations.

Screenings often include blood pressure, cholesterol, diabetes, cancer risk assessments, and age-specific tests based on your health profile.
